#About Us

Who We Are?

Autarky Kennels, founded in 2020, is a premier provider of highly trained security dogs based in Mutare, Zimbabwe. Our mission is to offer reliable and effective canine companions that enhance safety and security for homes and businesses alike. With a deep understanding of the unique needs of our community, we meticulously select and train each dog, ensuring they possess the ideal temperament and skills for protection. At Autarky Kennels, we are dedicated to fostering a strong bond between our dogs and their owners, empowering families and businesses to feel secure and confident. Trust us to provide you with a loyal guardian that will stand by your side.
